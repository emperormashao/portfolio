About Me

I am a Programme Coordinator at Youth Employment Service (YES) working on the Mastercard Foundation Associates Programme, where I coordinate youth placements, employer onboarding, and programme reporting. I am currently completing an Advanced Diploma in Labour Law at the University of the Western Cape and hold a Bachelor’s degree in Tourism Development and Management from the University of Johannesburg.

My longer-term goal is to become a labour dispute-resolution specialist and eventually a commissioner at institutions such as the CCMA or a bargaining council, focusing on fair processes, decent work, and the impact of technology on the future of work. I also build technology platforms such as GuildTap, a live MVP that digitises informal hiring in South Africa, and I am re-launching Mashao International as a holdings company for these ventures.

GuildTap – Informal Hiring Platform

Flagship

Founder & Full-Stack Product Owner

GuildTap is a live MVP platform that digitises informal hiring in South Africa, connecting clients and workers through a verified, experience-based marketplace.

  • Defined and implemented a clear marketplace flow: post job → upload proof → admin verifies → job opens → worker claims → worker completes → client approves → XP and payment status update.
  • Built key features including authentication and session persistence, admin legitimacy queue, and beta-readiness documentation using GitHub-based workflows.
  • Designed the platform to be lighter, faster to launch, and inclusive for local informal labour markets, with free access for workers and clients and direct payment flows.
